How to use these QR codes

Place one printed QR code at each station table. Attendees scan it after the 4-minute group discussion and submit their answers individually — the form opens directly on their phone.

To print: right-click this page → Print, or use Ctrl+P. Each QR card will print clearly at A4 size. For best results, print the QR codes at least 5cm × 5cm so phones can scan them from a distance.
